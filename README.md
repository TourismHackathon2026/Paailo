# 📖 About

**Paaila** is an AI-powered tourism companion designed specifically for Nepal.

Unlike conventional map applications that only provide navigation, Paaila combines intelligent trip planning, cultural exploration, local recommendations, accommodation discovery, and emergency assistance into a single platform.

Our goal is to make Nepal more accessible for both international and domestic tourists while promoting local businesses and lesser-known destinations.

---

# ❓ Problem Statement

Tourists visiting Nepal often rely on multiple applications and websites to plan and navigate their journey.

They must switch between:

- Google Maps for navigation
- Travel blogs for historical information
- Hotel booking platforms
- Restaurant review websites
- Social media for recommendations

This fragmented experience wastes time and often leaves travelers unaware of Nepal's hidden gems.

---

# 💡 Our Solution

Paaila provides an intelligent, centralized tourism platform that allows users to:

- Discover tourist destinations
- Learn historical and cultural significance
- Find nearby hotels and restaurants
- Generate personalized AI itineraries
- Explore local cuisine
- Navigate safely with emergency information

All within a single application.
